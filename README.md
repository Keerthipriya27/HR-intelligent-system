## 📖 Overview

Organizations often track payroll but lack visibility into **where employee time and costs are actually being spent**.

**HR Cost Intelligence Engine** is an AI-powered enterprise platform that transforms calendar activity into actionable workforce intelligence by automatically mapping meetings to projects, calculating HR expenditure, analyzing employee utilization, and visualizing real-time cost insights.

The platform enables HR teams, finance departments, and managers to make informed decisions using AI-driven analytics instead of manual spreadsheets.
